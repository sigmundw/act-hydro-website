Junhao Wang is a master's student at the University at Buffalo. He is passionate about hydrology and is currently focusing on improving the representation of snow physics in urban climate and hydrology models. He completed his undergraduate studies in Physical Geography and Resource Environment at Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology. Outside of academics, he enjoys photography, traveling, and exploring local culture.
